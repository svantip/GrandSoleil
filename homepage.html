Set in Rovinj, 1.3 km from Sand Beach Biondi and 1.6 km from Baluota Beach, Apartment Rovinj Grand Soleil offers a terrace and air conditioning. The accommodation is 1.1 km from the Porton Biondi Beach and features free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

The apartment with a balcony and garden views has 1 bedroom, a living room, a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen with an oven and a microwave, and 1 bathroom with a shower. Towels and bed linen are offered in the apartment.

A bicycle rental service is available at the apartment.

Popular points of interest near Apartment Rovinj Gran Solei include Cathedral St. Eufemia Rovinj, Balbi Arch and Rovinj Marina. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 38 km from the accommodation.
